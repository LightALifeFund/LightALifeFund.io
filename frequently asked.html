Is this a charity?

No, this isn't a charity. We are a mental health awareness organisation. Our aim is to raise awareness about mental health issues. We work hand in hand with many mental health charities and try to provide as much support to does who suffere from various mental health issues.

Where are you based?

We are based in Ireland but we help people all over the globe. We are people, one and the same. We work with many different charities worldwide.

Do you have any centres?

We have an office in Kishoge Community College, Lucan if you'd like to come down and have a chat! Otherwise, just give us a call or send us an email and we'll try reply asap.
