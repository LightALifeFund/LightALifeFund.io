Our core values

Our Mission

Mental Health Ireland promotes positive mental health and wellbeing to all individuals and communities, and through our network of Mental Health Associations, we support people who experience mental health difficulties on their journey of recovery.

Our Vision

Light a life's vision is for an Ireland where mental health is valued as being an essential part of personal wellbeing and the health of the nation. Our Values

We believe that everyone is entitled to inclusion in society and opportunity for personal fulfilment; We believe in the right of everyone to be treated with dignity, respect and equality; We believe in the right of those experiencing mental health difficulties to be supported in their recovery; We are committed to transparency in our work and compliance with the highest ethical standards.
